About Me

My name is Tanner Millican. I'm a freelance web developer from Eastland, Texas. I've had quite a few other jobs, but my most recent one was as a POS field technician. I've always had a great fondness for computers and technology in general.

That fondness for technology is what has lead me to the world of coding and web development. Having been a POS technician, I was able to get some hands on experience actually getting to work with technology (computers and company intranet mostly). But something was missing, I wasn't satisfied. And then I found the Coding Bootcamp at UT in Austin, Texas.

There, I've learned how to make quality, user-friendly, mobile-first responsive web sites that meet and exceed client expectations. Web sites that I've built utilize many technologies from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to Node.Js, Express.Js, MySQL, and others.
